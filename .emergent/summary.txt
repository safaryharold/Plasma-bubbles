### what currently exists?
A fully functional MVP of the IBP Analytics Platform is deployed. The system handles authentication (JWT), execution of IBP calculations (using a vectorized Numpy surrogate and Scikit-learn Gaussian Process Regressor for smoothing), background task queueing with Celery and Redis, and data storage in MongoDB. The frontend provides a dark-themed UI with pages for individual calculation, parameter sweeps, and scenario comparison. Data visualization is powered by Plotly.js, currently utilizing a 2D contour heatmap with a Viridis colorscale. Scenario sharing (public links) and NetCDF/Parquet exports are implemented.
